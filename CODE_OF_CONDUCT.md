Code of Conduct

This project is committed to a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free
environment for everyone.

Expected behavior
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus feedback on code and outcomes, not individuals.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions before escalating.

Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, intimidation, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated disruptive behavior after being asked to stop.

Enforcement
- Maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or block participation
  for behavior that violates this policy.
- Enforcement decisions prioritize community safety and project health.
